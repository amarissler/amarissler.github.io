Become a Substitute Teacher

The application for substitute teachers is currently open. The current schedule and application deadline dates are outlined below.

Your application must be completed and all required documents submitted by the application deadline date in order to be scheduled for the Substitute Orientation. Once you have completed your application and all required documents are on file, you will receive an email with fingerprint instructions and appointment details.

Upcoming Substitute Orientations Application Deadline Date

6/15/2016

 Each scheduled orientation has a maximum capacity and will close when filled or at least 10 days prior to the event.

Applicants are required to complete all parts of the hiring process listed below. Applicants who do not complete ALL requirements within the timeframe will need to reapply.

Required documents for substitute teaching are outlined below:

Application & Hiring Process:

        1. Submit an online application for the advertised substitute teacher job posting.

          The Broward County Substitute teacher job posting can be located under the category Substitute, under the "Vacancies" header. Select a Request to attend a Substitute orientation. Click here for a detailed description.

        2. Complete and upload the Substitute Teacher Training Certificate of Completion (This is a separate document from the Substitute Teacher Certificate).  Former substitute teachers, retired teachers, certified teachers, and education majors are exempt from this workshop. Proof is required (e.g. transcript indicating education major or teacher evaluation). Substitute Teacher Training may be completed at one of the following institutes for an approximate cost of $30.

              • Traditional training is available at Broward College http://www.broward.edu/academics/ce/Pages/Teacher-Education.aspx
              • Online training is available at Ed Training – Introduction to Teaching for Substitute Teacher  http://www.edtrainingcenter.com
        3. Transcripts from an accredited college showing at least 60 semester hours of completed course work.
        4. Two (2) letters of references are required from all applicants. References must be signed and dated. One reference must be from the candidate’s last employer or cooperating teacher (for education graduates). Each reference should be scanned separately and uploaded to the instructional application.
            • If you have limited or no previous work experience, personal references will be accepted. 
            • If you are currently attending a college/university, references should be from professors and/or instructors. 
        5. Substitute Teacher Certificate: $20.00 - Payment is required if applicant does not have a valid Florida Educator Certificate or Broward County Substitute Teacher Certificate. Select the payonline button below to complete this transaction.

        CLICK HERE to APPLY ONLINE for substitute teaching. Select the Broward County Substitute Teacher job at Talent Acquisition & Operations (Instructional) based on the orientation date.

Required Documents for Substitute Orientation: 
1. Original & Copy of  Social Security Card
2. Original & Copy of Photo ID (Current Driver's License, Passport, etc.)
3. Current Resident Alien Card or Work Permit (if applicable)
4. Voided Check. (You will be required to sign up for direct deposit)
        **IMPORTANT NOTICE**

Per Florida Statute 119.071(5), this is to notify you of the purpose for collecting and utilizing your Social Security Number. Providing your Social Security Number is a condition of employment at Broward County Public Schools. To protect your identification, Broward County Public Schools will secure your Social Security Number from unauthorized access, and strictly prohibit the release of your Social Security Number to unauthorized parties contrary to state and federal law.

Social Security numbers are collected for legitimate business purposes during the recruitment, selection and hiring process with Broward County Public Schools. Your Social Security Number will be used for completing and processing the following actions: Employment application for determining certification eligibility [1012.56 F.S.], Completion of federal I-9 form for the filing and issuance of federal employment forms [8 USC 1324a(b)], Completion of federal W-4 form for filing and issuance of federal employment forms [8 USC 1324a(b)], Florida Retirement Contribution Report [119.07(5)(a)6.b], and Conducting criminal background screening [1012.56 F.S.]. Your Social Security Number may be used as a unique numeric identification within some of our systems and may be used for search purposes.
